About Fuelerz

A Goa-based digital studio built for businesses that want to look ready online.

Fuelerz brings together design, websites, branding and clear digital presentation for brands that want stronger first impressions.

Founder

Sergio Caetano Fernandes

Born and raised in Goa, Sergio started Fuelerz with a simple belief: a business should not have to look ordinary just because it is starting small.

As a commerce student with a deep interest in technology, design and business growth, he built Fuelerz to help local brands move from basic online presence to polished digital identity. What began as a late-night idea has grown into a one-person creative studio serving businesses through websites, logos, graphics and brand systems.

Sergio is also an incoming BCA student at Fr. Agnel College, Pilar, after securing strong merit positions across leading BCA admissions in Goa, including St. Xavier’s College, Fr. Agnel College and Don Bosco College.
